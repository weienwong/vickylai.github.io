About Me

About Me

Hi there! I’m Vicky.

My first languages were English, CSS, and LPC. I spent whole summers as a little girl programming a text-based MUD over Telnet. I made all kinds of things in that fantasy world, but soon that wasn’t enough.

I started making things for the real world.

I fell hard for front end development, a relationship that’s held steady for a couple decades now. I’ve also learned to write JavaScript and Python, and I’m currently exploring Go. I aim to conquer full stack this year.

I don’t seem to have an “off” button when it comes to building things. I also like drinking lots of coffee. The latter seems to produce even more of the former.

To see what I’m currently up to, you can check out /now. You can also follow me on Twitter, where I’m @vickylaiio.

Have a really great day. :)

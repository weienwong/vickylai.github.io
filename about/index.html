About

Hello! I’m Vicky Lai, a full stack developer with experience in early-stage startups, UX and UI design, entrepreneurship, and team leadership consulting.

I designed and developed ApplyByAPI.com, a hiring tool that helps great companies hire awesome coders. I’m passionate about building products that make processes (and life!) easier.

I build software for web, desktop, and mobile. I’m typically language-agnostic, but most often code in Python, Go, and JavaScript.

I help companies of all sizes make their technology, operations, and people, even better. In partnership with Atazzo, I help growth-stage startups build scalable technology solutions. I love the feeling of successful collaboration that comes only from building up something great, together.
